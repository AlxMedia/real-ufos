Unknown lights flew in formation over Lubbock in 1951. Spotted by three professors and later photographed.

The skeptics viewpoint:

Skeptics often refer to one of the following explanations when it comes to UFOs:

  • Misidentified Aircraft
  • Space Debris
  • Weather Balloons
  • Atmospheric Phenomena
  • Light Aberrations
  • Hoaxes
  • Mass Hallucinations
  • Swamp Gas

These explainations may be correct in many cases. However, the question is: Are they always accurate, or are there exceptions?
